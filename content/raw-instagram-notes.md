  sesamsa.com (palette confirmed in browser inspector).

  - Body: clean sans (Jost on sesamsa.com).
- **Tone in captions:**

  - Short, evocative, slow-paced.
  - Heavy use of 🥐✨💛🎀 emoji as soft punctuation.

## 9. What this means for the landing page

- Page must read **luxury, calm, hand-crafted**, not "loud bakery chain".
- Lead with **Arabic RTL copy**; English appears as accent only.
- Lead with **bakery imagery** + **drinks** + **Eid/occasion boxes**.
- Use the brand colors (cream / sand / olive / dark espresso) derived from
  sesamsa.com, the official website of the same brand.
- All visual assets come from @sesam.sa posts only.
