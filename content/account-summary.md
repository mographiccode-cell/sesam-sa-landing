# SESAM — Account Summary

**@sesam.sa** is a Jeddah-based artisan bakery and café rooted in slow, hand-crafted
baking. The brand is the sister concept of Asian restaurant Yashi (`@yashi_ksa`), and
is co-founded by Chef **Ghofran Alromaihy**.

In 2026, SESAM was named **Best Bakery in Jeddah** at the **Time Out Jeddah** awards,
cementing its place in the city's growing specialty café scene.

The Instagram account (~35K followers, 155 posts) is the brand's primary visual
channel: warm cream / olive / sand photography, layered pastries, slow morning
imagery, signature drinks, and seasonal occasion boxes (notably the annual Eid
box). Bilingual AR / EN captions keep a refined but warm tone.

This landing page mirrors that identity — Arabic-first, RTL, cream + olive + sand
palette, real @sesam.sa imagery, no stock visuals.
